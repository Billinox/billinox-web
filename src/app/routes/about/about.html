About Billinox

Built to  Simplify Business Billing

Billinox is an offline-first invoice and billing platform crafted for freelancers, small businesses, agencies, and entrepreneurs. Our mission is simple — help every modern business run its billing without friction, anywhere, anytime.

Our mission

Billing software that respects your time and your data.

Billinox was created because traditional billing tools were heavy, expensive, and demanded constant connectivity. We believed every business — from a one-person consultancy to a growing agency — deserves a tool that is calm, fast, and works the way they do.

Offline-first is not a feature for us — it's a philosophy. Your business shouldn't stop because your network does.
